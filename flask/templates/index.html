About Us

Anemia is a condition where your body doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells or hemoglobin. Hemoglobin is the part of red blood cells that carries oxygen. When you’re anemic, your body doesn't get the oxygen it needs to function properly, which can make you feel tired or weak.

AnemiaSense is an AI-based health project that helps in the early detection of anemic diseases by analyzing key blood parameters like Hemoglobin, MCH, MCV, and MCHC. By using machine learning, it gives quick, reliable predictions, so you can take timely action and get the care you need — before symptoms get worse.
